PROJECT_03: FRAMEWORK

This document serves as the conceptual and technical framing for Project 1 (ERROR) and Project 2 (INFINITY). It transitions the work from the studio workshop into a curated portfolio mode, communicating intentions and outcomes to an audience beyond the studio.

PROJECT_01_&_02: THE_SOL_LEWITT_TOPICS

Projects 1 and 2 require the selection of one of the seven topics submitted by Sol LeWitt to David Askevold's Projects Class in 1969. The goal is to develop a substantial graphic design project where the work "uses the idea of" or "is" the topic, rather than merely illustrating it.

  • Conceptual Exploration: Research undertaken primarily through the act of making.
  • Substantial Artifacts: Production of tangible outcomes through sustained, ongoing studio practice.
  • Hybridized Practice: Graphic design as the shaping and organizing of images, information, and text within systems.
PROJECT_03: THE_FINAL_DOCUMENT

Project 3 focuses on the packaging and distribution of work for the reception of others. In graphic design a "ghost" discipline the work becomes the "other subjects" that must be designed into documents for communication.

This website functions as the Comprehensive Document for the course, shifting the work into the exhibition/publication mode.
